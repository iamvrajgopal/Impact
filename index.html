BTCP Modules: A Dual Approach to Future Readiness

BTCP Pillar 1: Foundational Science

Bringing Science to Life: The CCL Experiential Module

This module began with extensive research at the Center For Creative Learning (CCL) at IIT Gandhinagar, selecting 35 practical experiments from over 300 tools. These experiments cover key Grade 9 concepts—Kinematics, Newton's Laws, Electricity, Light, etc.—to ensure that students grasp the logic of science through hands-on discovery.

The core idea is to develop a new pedagogical approach that promotes the application of science in daily life, cultivating scientific curiosity and an entrepreneurial spirit early on.

BTCP Pillar 2: Future Skills Integration

AI & ML for Social Impact and Career Building

To build skills for the 21st century, I developed an AI/ML module for middle/secondary classes, based on best practices from international programs. The curriculum simplifies abstract topics and introduces students to free, online Scratch-based resources for immediate application.

The module's key feature is the project section, which focuses on socially relevant problem-solving. By ideating solutions and developing prototypes using AI/ML features, students connect computational power to real-world issues, helping them see the path to impactful careers and societal contribution.
